About Us

The Association of Kenyan Students in India (AKSI) is a community of students who are essentially Kenyan citizens pursuing their academic endeavors in India, with the essence of uniting all Kenyan students in the different states of India and fostering social and academic cooperation and interaction between its members and the larger world.
